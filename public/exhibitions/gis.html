Route mapping and optimization for solid waste management using GIS

An attempt was made by M.Tech student for selection of site for solid waste and optimization of best routes for collection and transportation using Remote Sensing Geographical Information System(GIS) and multi criteria Information approach. The same model is being used for design and optimization for various small towns.
