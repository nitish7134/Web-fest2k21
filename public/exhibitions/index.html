Socio-Tech Exhibition

Technology shapes society and society shapes technology. Technological innovation is not a panacea to social ailments, but rather a neutral tool that can propagate positive or negative outcomes depending on the circumstances in which technology is developed. When applied critically to certain social problems, technology can help to facilitate significant, positive social change. Ever heard of the term socio-tech before? The so-called socio-tech focus on how technology can be used to establish a best fit between the technical and social aspects of a society.
